About

Taekyu Yoon (EN) Taekyu Yoon, born in 1990 in Seoul, is a composer, arranger, pianist, and conductor. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in composition from Hanyang University in Seoul and continued his master’s studies at the University of Music and Theatre Leipzig with Prof. Fabien Lévy. Currently, he is pursuing a postgraduate degree in composition with Prof. Sidney Corbett, as well as a Master’s in New Music and Media Composition with Prof. Philipp Ludwig Stangl at the State University of Music and Performing Arts Mannheim. His music reflects not only musical ideas but also free thought and philosophical exploration. Yoon's works have been widely performed and recognized with international awards. He currently lives and works in Germany. Taekyu Yoon (KR) Taekyu Yoon, geboren 1990 in Seoul, ist ein Komponist, Arrangeur, Pianist und Dirigent. Er studierte Komposition an der Hanyang University in Seoul (BA) und setzte sein Masterstudium an der Hochschule für Musik und Theater Leipzig bei Prof. Fabien Lévy fort. Zurzeit absolviert er ein Zusatzstudium Komposition bei Prof. Sidney Corbett sowie den Master Neue Musik und Medienkomposition bei Prof. Philipp Ludwig Stangl an der Hochschule für Musik und Darstellende Kunst Mannheim.
