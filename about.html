Biography

About

Composer, arranger, pianist, and conductor working between contemporary music and new media composition.

Taekyu Yoon

Taekyu Yoon, born in 1990 in Seoul, is a composer, arranger, pianist, and conductor.

Postgraduate degree (Meisterklassenexamen) in Composition, University of Music and Performing Arts Mannheim

Master’s degree in Composition, University of Music and Theatre Leipzig

Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Composition, Hanyang University College of Music

Currently enrolled in the Master’s program in New Media Composition, University of Music and Performing Arts Mannheim
