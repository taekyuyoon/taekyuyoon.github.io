About

Taekyu Yoon, born in 1990 in Seoul, is a composer, arranger, pianist, and conductor. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in composition from Hanyang University in Seoul and continued his master’s studies at the University of Music and Theatre Leipzig with Prof. Fabien Lévy. Currently, he is pursuing a postgraduate degree in composition with Prof. Sidney Corbett, as well as a Master’s in New Music and Media Composition with Prof. Philipp Ludwig Stangl at the State University of Music and Performing Arts Mannheim.

His music reflects not only musical ideas but also free thought and philosophical exploration. Yoon's works have been widely performed and recognized with international awards. He currently lives and works in Germany.
