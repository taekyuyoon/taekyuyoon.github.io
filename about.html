About

Taekyu Yoon (EN)

Taekyu Yoon, born in 1990 in Seoul, is a composer, arranger, pianist, and conductor. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in composition from Hanyang University in Seoul and continued his master’s studies at the University of Music and Theatre Leipzig with Prof. Fabien Lévy. Currently, he is pursuing a postgraduate degree in composition with Prof. Sidney Corbett, as well as a Master’s in New Media Composition with Prof. Philipp Ludwig Stangl at the State University of Music and Performing Arts Mannheim.

His music reflects not only musical ideas but also free thought and philosophical exploration. Yoon's works have been widely performed and recognized with international awards. He currently lives and works in Germany.

Selected Awards

  • Winner of the 5th International "Andrea Ceraso" Rome Award Composition Competition
  • 2nd Prize, II International Composition Competition of the Protestant Church of Zepernick-Schönow (Randfestspiele, Germany)
  • Selected Composer, Daegu International Contemporary Music Festival (South Korea)
  • 2nd Prize, Biennale Composition Competition "La Gnove Musiche" (Italy)
  • 3rd Prize, 36th International Composition Competition for Orchestra by the Korean Music Association for Overseas Koreans (South Korea)
  • ARKO Project Orchestra Scholarship (approx. €8,000, South Korea)
  • Final Scholarship Recipient, ARKO Academy of Art Creation (€15,000 grant, premiere at Lotte Concert Hall, Seoul)

Other Artistic Activities

  • Pianist & musical director, European Union Praise Teams at KOSTA Europe
  • Arranger & conductor, Association for Korean Reunification (Leipzig)
  • Arranger, Ordi Ensemble (South Korea)

Taekyu Yoon (DE)

Taekyu Yoon, geboren 1990 in Seoul, ist ein Komponist, Arrangeur, Pianist und Dirigent. Er studierte Komposition an der Hanyang University in Seoul (BA) und setzte sein Masterstudium an der Hochschule für Musik und Theater Leipzig bei Prof. Fabien Lévy fort. Zurzeit absolviert er ein Zusatzstudium(Meisterklasseexamen) Komposition bei Prof. Sidney Corbett sowie den Master Neue Medien Komposition bei Prof. Philipp Ludwig Stangl an der Hochschule für Musik und Darstellende Kunst Mannheim.

Seine Musik ist Ausdruck seiner Ideen, freien Gedanken und philosophischen Haltung. Yoons Werke wurden vielfach aufgeführt und international ausgezeichnet. Aktuell lebt und arbeitet er in Deutschland.

윤태규 (KR)

작곡가 윤태규는 1990년 서울에서 태어난 작곡가이자 편곡가, 피아니스트, 지휘자이다. 그는 한양대학교에서 작곡을 전공하며 학사 학위를 취득한 후, 독일 라이프치히 국립음대에서 파비앙 레비 교수 사사로 석사 과정을 마쳤다. 현재는 만하임 국립음대에서 시드니 코벳 교수에게 작곡을, 필리프 루트비히 슈탕엘 교수에게 뉴미디어 작곡 석사 과정을 병행하며 공부하고 있다.

그의 음악은 단순한 음악적 아이디어를 넘어서 자유로운 사유와 철학적 탐구를 표현하는 것을 목표로 한다. 윤태규의 작품은 국내외에서 활발히 연주되었으며, 다양한 국제 작곡상 수상 경력을 가지고 있다. 현재 그는 독일에 거주하며 창작 활동을 이어가고 있다.
